Experiences

I have strong curiosity about the world with a focus on human-environmental interactions.

  • 2008-2011

    Master Student

    I focused on pylogeography of the oriental oak in East Asia while I participated multiple research projects. I aslo learned landscape architecture and planning.

  • 2011-2013

    Project Manager

    I first worked full time on supply chain management in industry. I then managed differnt research, teaching and consulting projects for part-time jobs.

  • 2013-2015

    Erasmus Mundus Scholar

    I was funded to study on forestry and natural resource management in three Mediterranean countries (Portugal, Spain and Italy). I earned a joint master degree.

  • 2015-2019

    Doctoral Researcher

    I completed a doctoral dissertation on exploring the cross-scale effects of climate change and forest governance on large-scale forest disturbance. I also worked on urban environmental problems.
