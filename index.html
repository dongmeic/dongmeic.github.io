Interests

I enjoy managing data and programming for environmental problem-solving and social inquiry

Visualization

Using data science and spatial analysis to visualize and analyze spatial and temporal patterns and relationships among variables

Sustainability

Studying with decision-making goals of sustainable management of resources, social equity and bridging science-policy gaps

Complexity

Understanding nonlinear patterns emerged from cross-scale interactions and feedback loops and decomposing uncertainty

Projects

I explore both large-scale landscape patterns and city-scale problems

Climate space

How has species climatic niche changed during geographical expansion?

Species distribution modeling

How has climate change contributed to species distribution?

Forest management

How has forest management affected disturbance patterns?

Water quality

How has green infrastructure impacted water quality?

Social equity

What is the relationship between green infrastructure and equity?

Air quality

What are the rates, pathways and determinants of air quality?

Experiences

I have strong curiosity about the world with a focus on human-environmental interactions

  • 2008-2011

    Graduate Research Fellow

    I focused on pylogeography of the oriental oak in East Asia while I was participating multiple research projects on plant ecology. I also took courses on landscape architecture and planning.

  • 2011-2013

    Project Manager

    I worked full time on supply chain management in the retail industry for 19 months, and then managed part-time different research, teaching and consulting projects for 10 months.

  • 2013-2015

    Erasmus Mundus Scholar

    I was funded to study courses on forestry and natural resource management in three Mediterranean countries (Portugal, Spain and Italy). I earned a joint master degree.

  • 2015-2019

    Doctoral Researcher

    I completed a doctoral dissertation on exploring the cross-scale effects of climate change and forest governance on large-scale forest disturbance. I also worked on urban environmental problems.
